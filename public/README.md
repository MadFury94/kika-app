# Image & Video Assets

Place your Kika Oshodin images and video in this folder with the following names:

## Required Images:

1. **kika-hero.jpg** (Main featured image)
   - Recommended size: 800x1200px (portrait)
   - This will be the large featured image in the gallery
   - Best quality image of Kika

2. **kika-1.jpg** (Gallery image 1)
   - Recommended size: 600x600px (square)
   - Could show Kika with children or at an event

3. **kika-2.jpg** (Gallery image 2)
   - Recommended size: 600x600px (square)
   - Could show Kika's work in the community

## Required Video:

**palliative.mp4** (Outreach video)
- Format: MP4 (H.264 codec recommended)
- Recommended resolution: 1920x1080 (Full HD) or 1280x720 (HD)
- The video will play in a beautiful player with custom controls
- Shows your 2024 outreach program in action

## Image Guidelines:

- Format: JPG or PNG
- Quality: High resolution for best display
- The images will be automatically optimized by Next.js
- Images will have a beautiful overlay with captions

## Video Guidelines:

- Format: MP4 (most compatible)
- Codec: H.264 for best browser support
- Keep file size reasonable (under 50MB recommended)
- The video player includes play/pause controls

## Current Status:

- Images: Until you add them, placeholder icons will be displayed
- Video: Until you add palliative.mp4, the placeholder with play button will show

## How to Add Assets:

1. Save your images and video with the exact names above
2. Place them in this `public` folder
3. Refresh your browser - they will appear automatically!
